Calculate Your Student Grade

Our Student Grade Calculator takes a percentage mark for each of your assignments or modules, together with the percentage weighting, and returns a final grade for the assignments you have completed so far.

How Do I Calculate My Student Assignment Grade?

To work out your student grade for your module, or assignment, we take the marks (or grades) multiplied by their respective weights, sum them together, and then divide the total by the sum of the weights.

Student A is studying a degree in Computer Science and he is calculating three assignments so far. They want to calculate their final grade for a certain module.
